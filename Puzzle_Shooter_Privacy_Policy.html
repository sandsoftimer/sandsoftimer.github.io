Privacy Policy — Puzzle Shooter

FunFox Studio — Last updated: December 2025

FunFox Studio ("we", "our", "us") operates the mobile game Puzzle Shooter (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App we may automatically collect non-personal information to improve and maintain the game. This may include:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage data (session length, screens visited)
  • Crash logs and performance data
  • General analytics and interaction data

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, postal address, or phone number.

2. Third-Party Services

We use AppLovin to deliver ads and help monetize the App. AppLovin may collect the following information in accordance with its own policies:

  • Advertising identifiers (such as AAID or IDFA)
  • App usage and behavioral data
  • IP address (processed temporarily)

AppLovin's use of data is governed by AppLovin's privacy policy: https://www.applovin.com/privacy.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Improve gameplay, performance, and stability
  • Analyze how players interact with the App
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Display ads via AppLovin and optimize ad performance
  • Maintain core App functionality

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Children’s Privacy

Puzzle Shooter is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete that information.

5. Advertising & Tracking

The App shows ads served by AppLovin. AppLovin may use device identifiers and other signals to serve and personalize ads, prevent fraud, and measure ad performance. Where required by law (for example, in the EU under GDPR), the App may request your consent to personalized advertising.

You can opt out of ad tracking via your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect the information we collect. However, no electronic transmission or storage method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights including:

  • The right to request access to data we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of targeted advertising

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the address below. Some rights may also be exercised via your device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
